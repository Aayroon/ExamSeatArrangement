Exam Seating Arrangement: Quick Guide

Welcome to the Exam Hall Seating Arrangement System developed to simplify the process of examination hall allotment and seating arrangement for your college. This quick guide will help you navigate through the key functionalities of the system.

For Administrators
  • ADMIN LOGIN
    1. Register yourself on clicking Sign Up.
    2. Log In with registered username and password.
    3. Enter room details for the upcoming examination.
    4. A seating chart will be automatically generated based on the provided information.
  • UPDATE

    Modify seating arrangements by changing particular roll numbers with others.

  • VIEW

    Easily view the seating arrangement for a specific examination room.


    • For students.
    1. Click on Student.
    2. Enter your roll number.
    3. Your allotted seat will be displayed if the seating chart has been prepared.

    For Invigilators.
    1. Click on Faculty.
    2. Enter your name.
    3. Your allotted examination room and timing will be displayed.

    Note: Currently, the system supports up to 8 columns in a room.
